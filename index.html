Welcome to Buzzibodies

About

This friendly group runs on Tuesday mornings (term time only) from 9.30-11.30am. There are lots of toys, space to run around, a craft activity and song time each week. Plus we have lots of special events at various times in the year such as a music group, “Shake, Rattle and Roll”, pottery painting, seasonal parties, messy play and a local photographer. It's a great way for parents/carers and their children to meet each other in a relaxed and fun atmosphere. It's priced at £2 per child, 50p for additional children. A drink and snack is also included for both adults and children.

What We Do

Free Play

We have a wide variety of toys including cars, play kitchen, train sets, dolls, dressing up, Happy Land, giant Jenga, Skuttle Bugs, baby toys and mats, books, puzzles, etc for the children to use for free play.

Craft & Messy Play

Each week we have a craft activity for the children to engage with. From time to time we have a session dedicated to Messy Play with several craft activities running together.

Singing

Whilst the toys are being tidied away, we wrap up each session with a 15min group singing session. The children can make use of various musical instruments to play along as well as singing.

Refreshments

We have tea, coffee and biscuits for the adults, and the children are offered water, juice, fruit and biscuits.

Activities

We often have activities in addition to the regular free play, craft activity and singing. These have included: parachute games; a football activities; pottery making etc.

Visits

From time to time we have visits to the session. These have included: Father Christmas; Vintage Bus; Fire Engine; Tractor etc.
